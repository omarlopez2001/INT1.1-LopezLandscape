About Us

Lopez Landscape and Gardesign is a family-run business by father and son, Isidro and Omar Lopez. Isidro Lopez has 20+ years of experience in landscaping. Over the last few years, he has shared his skills and expertise with Omar to help create and grow a successful business.

Lopez Landscape and Gardesign specialize in providing quality landscape design and maintenance. We strive to be customer service orientated and work closely with our clients to fulfill their goals. As a team, we're able to design and build our clients' ideas and wishes into reality. Our boundless knowledge and expertise on plants, flower and sod installation, retaining walls, patios, fences, and drainage compliment our clients' objectives and maximizes their investment.
